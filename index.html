About TrustEye

Your AI-Powered Threat Detection Partner

TrustEye empowers individuals and organizations to identify and prevent cyber threats before they cause damage. Our advanced AI analyzes emails, URLs, and files to detect phishing, malware, and other security risks in real-time.

What TrustEye Does

  • Email Analysis: Detects phishing attempts, suspicious links, and malicious attachments
  • URL Scanning: Identifies malicious websites, IP-based attacks, and URL shortener risks
  • File Security: Analyzes documents for embedded threats and suspicious content
  • Real-time Protection: Instant threat assessment with confidence scoring

Our Story

TrustEye was born from a simple observation: cyber threats are evolving faster than traditional security solutions can adapt.

Developed for Smart India Hackathon 2024, our team recognized that individuals and small organizations often lack access to enterprise-grade threat detection. We set out to democratize cybersecurity by creating an AI-powered solution that's both powerful and accessible.
